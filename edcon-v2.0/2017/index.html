How to get there?

Travel by RATP public transportation, bus or metro in Paris is easy and inexpensive.
The nearest metro station is Rue Saint-Maur (line 3) which is at the doorstep of our building.
You may download the very practical RATP application here, for Android, and here, for Iphone.
By bike: there are several stations of the Paris public self-service bike hire system near the School: Vélib’.
The Vélib application is for download here, for Android, and here, for Iphone.

École Supérieure de Commerce de Paris is a business school established in 1819 in Paris as the world's first business school. It has campuses in Berlin, London, Madrid, Paris, Turin and Warsaw and it is one of the best business schools in the world. Accredited by the Paris Chamber of Commerce, ESCP Europe is one of the 64 schools in the world that has the triple accreditation of AACSB, EQUIS and AMBA. ESCP Europe aims to prepare high-level students for top executive functions and it offers to its students a range of dual degrees and exchange programs with academic institutions from all over the world. The school's notable alumni in business and politics include Jean-Pierre Raffarin (the former French Prime Minister), Olivier Blanchard (former IMF Chief Economist), Christophe de Margerie (former CEO of Total) and so on. The ESCP Master in Management is consistently regarded by the Financial Times as one of the best in the world and it placed ESCP's Master in Finance 2nd and its Executive MBA 12th in 2016 worldwide. ESCP Master programs cover Finance, Management and so on and it also has PhD programs to train research-active professors and high-level consultants in the field of Management Science.

This conference focuses on technology, innovation, finance and targets for better communication and cooperation between developers, startups, investors and Efans. The conference would not be the success that it is without the support of ESCP, we would like to give our sincere thanks for ESCP’s help and cooperation.
