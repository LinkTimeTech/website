About

Asia-Pacific Ethereum Community Meetup is organized by Linktime. It aims at promoting technology exchange and connecting Ethereum developers to community members. The guests we invited include the founder of Ethereum, Ethereum developers, Ethereum reseacher, founders of excellent Ethereum-based projects, noted investors and enterprise representatives in this space, etc.

The meetup is specifically located in 19F, Unit 1, Building A, Smart Plaza, 4068 Qiaoxiang Road, Nanshan District, Shenzhen, China. The Window of the Word, Happy Valley, Splendid China Folk Culture Village are featured attractions around the location. Besides, we have prepared professional simultaneous interpretation during the event for the needs of different languages, and a tea break is also available during the event.
