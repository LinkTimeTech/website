Ethereum Founder Vitalik Buterin Was named as One of Bloomberg’s Top 50 Most Influential People

“From finance and politics to technology and culture, there is always people to promote the whole industry development from all walks of life. Among the 50 top influential figures released by the Bloomberg, Vitalik Buterin, the founder of Ethereum , takes one of the seats.”

Recently, Bloomberg has issued the list of the 50 most influential people of the year, and this list includes Vitalik Buterin, founder of Ethereum.

The list includes some of the most influential movers and shakers of the past year in all walks of life. For instance, Amazon founder and CEO Jeff Bezos, CEO of both SpaceX and Tesla, Musk, and Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman.

It is enumerated that, in nowadays, there are over 700 different blockchain-based cryptocurrencies. The Ethereum has swept through the digital currency market since its release. Its total market value reached about $944 million by October 31 this year.

The cryptocurrency market is growing explosively, bringing Bitcoin and Ethereum into the mainstream. In recent days, the price of each Ethereum has exceeded $470, reaching an all time high point.

Vitalik Buterin, the 23-year-old boy, is shocking the financial industry with the revolutionary technology. At the same time, he is producing great waves to the start-up companies and programmers around the world. The rethought technology platform is gaining momentum in the crowdfunding and financial sourcing industries.

According to the list makers: “Yet the potential for Ethereum to upend how computers move money continues to attract Wall Street’s attention. Already it’s altering how companies fund development: Ether is a popular vehicle for buying into initial coin offerings, or ICOs, which bypass the traditional routes of venture capital and initial public offerings. It’s helped blockchain startups raise more than $3 billion this year."

Buterin said that we need to create a technology that allows people to explore and create many interesting things. With this technology, everything could be changed.
