BCH=BTC？While Ethereum Founder Vitalik Buterin Doesn't Think So！

"Between BTC and BCH, a seemingly white war is at a trigger. Both blockchains are seeking for establishing dominance. For a long time to come, BCH will remain controversial. And what might happen in the future, let’s go and see. "

Recently, Bitcoin (BTC) and Bitcoin cash (BCH) are among the hottest discussions in the digital currency field, as BCH recently surges to a record high at $ 1,000 and ranks second in the market value. Besides, BCH also gradually catches up with Ethereum in transaction volume, and once even surpassed Bitcoin, which proves it is accepted by a large amount of users. Even Vitalik Buterin, the founder of Ethereum, also tweeted to congratulate BCH.

However, when some people refer to BCH as BTC, Vitalik Buterin said in a recent Twitter claim that BCH is not equal to BTC and for the moment it can not be confused.

The view that Bitcoin cash (BCH) can be regarded as Bitcoin is not accepted by most cryptocurrency users, but this view is not worthless. BCH not only has the function of value storage, but also can bring users an instant and time-saving experience with a low fee requirement as a fast-paying currency. Many early Bitcoin users say that BCH is a tool for value storage and exchange.

At present, both BTC and BCH are striving for establishing dominance. In the sight of the project development, Bitcoin is still ahead. The price of BCH has gone up sharply, and many supporters believe it represents the "natural needs". But others regard it as the largest-scale pump and dump in the cryptocurrency sector.

In Vitalik's Twitter, he considered BCH as a legitimate contender for the Bitcoin name. He also mentioned that Bitcoin itself is not perfect: although the community has been supporting SegWit for more than a year, no reasonable scaling plan is currently available; in addition, it is difficult for many companies to integrate this plan due to the complexity of Segwit. Vitalik believed that the periodic on-chain block scaling is a question worth considering, and the censorship by Theymos (/ r / Bitcoin moderators) is also a big issue.

For now, BCH is not and can not replace BTC, which Vitalik also agreed with. Obviously, the situation of BCH has caused a lot of confusions. While for a long time to come, BCH will still be controversial.
