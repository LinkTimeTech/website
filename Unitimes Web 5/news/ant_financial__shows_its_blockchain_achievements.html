Ant Financial Services Shows its Blockchain Achievements at the Fourth World Internet Conference

“As long as the license plate is tied to Alipay, visitors can smoothly drive out without a stop or paying in cash, even without the need to take out the phone.”

On December 3, the Fourth World Internet Conference officially opened in Wuzhen, East of China’s Zhejiang Province. The theme of the conference is “Developing the Digital Economy, Promoting Openness and Sharing - Join Hands to Build a Community with Common Destiny”. It is reported that this conference attracted more than 1,500 participants, and many of the world’s leading technology companies and brands released their latest achievements at the conference. Besides, what makes people excited is that the Ant Financial Services Group (established on October 16, 2014) also showed its blockchain achievements at the conference, which is really impressive.

It’s reported that the Sesame Credit under Ant Financial Services added 64 power bank renting and returning outlets in the entire Wuzhen Xizha scenic area. As long as the sesame points stand at 600 points or more, users can borrow the power banks free of guarantee deposit, and return to any outlets providing power bank renting services throughout the country (more than 200,000 outlets) within 7 days.

In addition, its “Face-recognition payment” has also been applied in the parking lot of Wuzhen Dongzha scenic area. As long as the license plate is tied to Alipay, visitors can smoothly drive out without a stop or paying in cash, even without taking out the phone. Behind the “Face-recognition payment” experience is the image recognition technology based on the smart device, which enables that the license plate recognition and money deductions are done in one run.

It is known that biometric identification is one of the core technologies of Ant Financial. In October 2017, Ant Financial Services set up ZOLOZ, a globally trusted identification platform, to open up financial biometric identification to the world and help users solve the “who are you?” problem in digital age.

Above-mentioned achievements have been scored based on blockchain. On July 31, 2017, Cheng Li, CTO of Ant Financial Services, announced at the 2016 Internet Finance Bund Summit that Ant Financial Services will apply blockchain technology to the scenario of public benefit to raise funds for those children with hearing impairment so that each and every donation can be open and transparent, immutable and traceable.

In November 2017, the ant blockchain technology was used in food safety tracking and products authenticity tracing. At present, users can scan the milk powder produced in Australia and New Zealand and Maotai made in China through Alipay to tell the authenticity.

Ant Financial Services is a financial service platform focused on serving “grass-root” consumers and small and micro enterprises. Businesses it operates include Alipay, Alipay Wallet, Yu’e Bao, Zhao Cai Bao, Ant Credit and MYbank.
