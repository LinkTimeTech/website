Asia-Pacific Ethereum Community Meetup Successfully Taken Place in Shenzhen, China

“Blockchain Technology is seen as another mainstream trend apart from mobile internet. Its properties of decentralization, trustlessness, tamper-resistance have unparalleled advantages over traditional technologies. "

In recent years, especially in the year of 2017, despite the fluctuations in price, digital currencies like bitcoin and ethereum attracted more and more eyeballs from the public, and an increasingly number of people become Blockchain and Ethereum fans and begin to study the behind blockchain technology.

Yesterday (Dec 3), Asia-Pacific Ethereum Community Meetup was Successfully Taken Place in Shenzhen, China. Participants come from not only Chinese ethereum communities, but also the various communities worldwide, besides, medias in this space like 8btc.com and Leiphone.com and some others were also come to report. This event was organized by Linktime, aims at promoting technology exchange and connecting Ethereum developers with community members, as well as creating opportunities for users to know more about the Ethereum-based projects.

Apart from the pre-registered participants, the meetup also received many unexpected ‘airdroppers’ from Beijing, HongKong, Singapore, etc on the day, thus we can see obviously the popularity of ethereum among the public.

 The founder of linktime is giving an opening speech, and say there will be more offline meetups in the coming future

The event covers the Ethereum-based technology training, excellent use case discussion, and the current development of Asia-Pacific Ethereum communities, etc, and noted guests were invited to give speeches, including the founder of Ethereum,Vitalik Buterin; American Ethereum researcher, Jon Choi; Tai Wan Ethereum researchers, Hsiao-wei Wang and Nicolas Lin; founder of KyberNetwork, Loi Luu; Special advisor of OmiseGo, Thomas Greco; Partner of Hashed（Korea）,Simon Kim; founder of EverMarkets, Jim Bai; founder of Olympus Labs, Kai Chen; and Consensys-MetaMask Software Engineer, Kevin Serrano; Consensys-Uport Product Lead, Michael Sena; COO of huobi, Robin Zhu; Economic researcher of MakerDAO,Chao Pan; founder of Bluzelle, Pavel Bains; partner of Kenetic Capital, Jehan Chu. Many participants at the meetup were from China, Korea, Singapore and America. During the event, the speakers interact and exchange ideas with audiences around technology and community development.

In the afternoon, Vitalik Buterin gave a speech on how will Ethereum protect privacy and share details in-depth with audiences and blockchain technology researchers both at home and abroad.

Currently, Ethereum has active developer communities and a blockchain infrastructure to meet the needs of business. This community represents tens of thousands of developers and ethereum fans from all over the world with the aim of making blockchain technology better and faster and enabling more and more people to deeply understand blockchain technology so as to truly bring benefits to society in near future.

After the event, according to participants, they have gained a lot of experience and technical knowledge in this space and are very grateful to the organizer. At the same time, they also expressed their expectation for participating in the next meetup.
