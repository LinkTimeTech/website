Rofex, Argentina’s Largest Futures Market Plans to provide Custody Services for Bitcoin

“Argentina’s Largest Futures Market has initially drawn up the plan to offer investors bitcoin service. ”

Argentina’s Largest Futures Market has initially drawn up the plan to offer investors bitcoin service.

Mercado de Termino de Rosario (Rofex), the largest futures market in Argentina, is focusing on how to provide crypto futures as part of its offerings. Rofex has preliminarily proposed to offer digital asset custody services and allow clients to use bitcoin as collateral in futures trading. According to Rofex, the possible offering is at “laboratory stage” and the company plans to release an official announcement “before the end of the year”. For mainstream financial institutions, cryptocurrencies are increasingly regraded as another way to extend business lines.

In regards to the reason for providing the service, Rofex said, “It’s our core business. The company would still need to seek regulatory approval before launching any bitcoin product.”

Last week, Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME Group), the world’s largest futures firm, has announced that it would launch bitcoin futures contracts in the fourth quarter of this year. The plan is pending regulatory approval.

Bitcoin’s price has rocketed substantially since the end of October, repeatedly setting new all-time highs. Driven by the US, Japan and South Korea, three largest exchange markets, the price has risen to $7,500.
