Kyber Network Partners With imToken

China’s leading mobile wallet app just got more im-pressive.

China’s leading mobile wallet app just got more im-pressive.

We are referring to none other than imToken and the newly announced collaboration with Kyber Network to integrate platforms for instant exchange services.

The imToken wallet, available on Android and iOS devices, has more than 350,000 users worldwide and boasts features such as embedded DApps, multi-token support, and transaction push notifications. At the recent Beyond Block Conference in Taipei, He Bin, CEO and founder of the Hangzhou-based startup, sweetened the pot by officially introducing the upcoming imToken 2.0, to be launched as early as January 2018. The new version of the wallet will not only have a cross-chain architecture to support Bitcoin wallets, it will also provide a gateway service for decentralized exchanges — which is where Kyber Network comes in. The partnership will integrate our protocol with the imToken wallet, essentially allowing users to make instant token conversions without ever having to leave the comfort and familiarity of their wallet. This integration guarantees liquidity for imToken users and allows them to seamlessly manage a variety of digital assets.

Kyber Network in imToken

Integration with existing wallet apps and increasing their conversion capabilities is critical for Kyber Network as it facilitates adoption and builds network effect. Moreover, imToken’s strong presence in China, where we also have a large customer base, will drive up awareness of our instant exchange services and consolidate market penetration. We hope you’re as excited about this partnership as we are!

To stay up-to-date with our announcements and progresses, please join our Telegram group (https://t.me/kybernetwork) or search for “kybernetwork” on Telegram.

About Kyber Network

Kyber Network is a decentralized and trustless exchange that facilitates instant conversion of crypto-assets with guaranteed liquidity. Our protocol will be key to promote liquidity of crypto-assets in the blockchain ecosystem, and improve the perception of tokens as a tradable asset class to the greater public.

About imToken

One of the largest wallet providers in the world, imToken is a simple yet powerful light-wallet that supports multi-tokens, third-party DApps, HDWallet, address book, transaction push notifications, and access to exchanges. Keys are encrypted locally in both hot and cold modes, making the wallet highly secure and private.
