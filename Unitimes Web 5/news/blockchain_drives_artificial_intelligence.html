Blockchain Drives Artificial Intelligence? NEO Announces Its Strategic Investment in Deep Brain Chain

“From Go to human speech recognition, researchers in the field of artificial intelligence (AI) have made great achievements in recent years. In short, big data has changed AI, and blockchain technology also expects to transform AI in its own way.”

Recently, NEO, a notable distributed smart asset platform in the world, has announced its strategic investment in Deep Brain Chain to promote the application of blockchain technology in the process of developing artificial intelligence.

As a world-renown distributed smart asset platform, NEO enables developers of blockchain technology to build projects and develop more blockchain applications based on the NEO smart asset contracts.

Deep Brain Chain is a platform to create decentralized AI computing via the combination of blockchain technology and AI technology. Its one of the earliest teams focusing on Natural Language Processing (NLP) in China. According to the team, artificial intelligence includes three sections: algorithms, computing power and data. After some researches, they found that the blockchain smart contracts, decentralization and other features could help break the bottlenecks that artificial intelligence would encounter in the development.

As for how to meet the demands of the artificial intelligence industry for data via blockchain, the team said first and foremost we need to understand the common issues faced by the AI industry and conduct in-depth studies on related technologies; second, we need to understand the concept of blockchain and cryptographic technologies; and we also need to know how to combine artificial intelligence technology, cryptography, distributed ledger and cloud computing technology to find solutions towards the issues we meet.

It is reported that NEO will work with Deep Brain Chain on the project promotion, developer sharing in major communities and the promotion to overseas communities to apply blockchain technology into more scenarios.
