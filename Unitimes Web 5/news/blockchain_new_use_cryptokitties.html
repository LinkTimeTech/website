Blockchain New Use: CryptoKitties

“The combination of game application and blockchain marks that the blockchain industry has entered a historic phase.”

Recently, the CryptoKitties, which worth a sky-high price, are the focused topic among the entire blockchain and cryptocurrency circles, and also have been discussing on the Internet everywhere!

CryptoKitties is a virtual kitten breeding game made by the AxiomZen team based San Francisco studio. It is also called the third-biggest killer app apart from Bitcoin and Ethereum smart contract. These kitties can produce offspring, be purchased and sold, but they can not be copied and burned, which make them the first virtual pets to be completely owned by users.

Unlike the pet-breeding model in QQ farm (a web game produced by Tencent), these CryptoKitties are not just virtual kittens, but also blockchain ones. Since all purchases, sales, and other information are recorded on blockchain, the operation information is trackable. The CryptoKitties is also a part of personal digital asset that anyone can buy with ethers.

What makes people excited is that users can also make money by selling their own CryptoKitties. With auction mechanism, the owners can set an initial price and an end price. As time goes by, the price will keep dropping until someone buys the CroptoKitties. In addition, kittens can be created by breeding, and there is a “cooldown time” after each breeding, and the cooldown time may last from one hour to a week, of course the shorter the better, since you can sell the offspring sooner and breed again.

It is reported that each kitten has a 256-bit genome that holds the genetic sequence to all the different combinations that kittens can have, including their beards, stripes, breeding time, background color and so on, so all the kittens are unique. Some of these genes are even recessive. To ensure their values, rarity of these combinations also has been taken into account, since kittens with rare genetic traits will be sold at a high price.

It’s easy to be involved in CryptoKitties game, as long as you buy Ethereum in the open market, download the MetaMask plugin of google chrome, and then create an account at CryptoKitties.co. Users only need two different kittens which will breed and give birth to a new kitty. These kitties can continue to breed, or be sold in the market.

And just a few days after CryptoKitties was launched, people had spent more than $ 6 million on it and the number is still growing. The ethereum network was clogged seriously. The CryptoKitties took over 17% of Ethereum’s network in just a few hours, surpassing EtherDelta, the major decentralized exchange. Besides, it is reported that the SophiaTX ICO planned to be launched on the Dec 5 has also been delayed.

The advent of CryptoKitties is much painful for those who want to participate in crowdfunding or other transactions. However, in the long run, it’s definitely a good thing for promoting the development of this space and accelerating Ethereum innovation.
