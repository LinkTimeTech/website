SBI Ripple Asia to Test Blockchain Bank Transfers Between Japan and South Korea

12 / 9 / 2017

Editor: Xue Xu

SBI Ripple Asia 将在日本与韩国进行区块链银行转账测试

Editor: Xue Xu

12th Sept. 2017

SBI Ripple Asia will reportedly begin testing a funds-transfer system using digital ledger technology (DLT) between Japanese and South Korean banks by the end of 2017.

SBI Ripple Asia is a joint venture between Tokyo-based financial services firm SBI and Ripple, which is a DLT payments startup. Earlier this year, the company has already led a consortium of banks to complete a pilot implementation with Ripple's technology within Japan. SBI Ripple Asia will work alongside DAYLI Intelligence (blockchain and AI solutions provider) to expand the program into South Korea, according to the local media.

The company is also planning a program currently, which will commence in October, to train engineers from about 20 blockchain and cryptocurrency technologies companies, including Nomura Research Institute, Toppan Printing and NEC.
