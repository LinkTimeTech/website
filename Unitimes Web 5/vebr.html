Chinese Internet Giant Tencent Issued Blockchain White Paper

Editor: Xue Xu

24th April, 2017

Chinese internet conglomerate Tencent issued a blockchain white paper in the first half of this year, aiming at working with its partners to push forward the development of a reliable internet and achieve a win-win situation. Meanwhile, the Tencent bockchain industry solutions with independent intellectual property rights was also released on its official website - trustsql.qq.com.

The blockchain can achieve the security of data and make the data unchangeable, build trust among peers and reduce the cost via decentralization. In the future, the blockchain will lead the internet of information and move towards the internet of value, reflecting the value of internet, achieving a transparent transfer and creating a trustworthy society.

Late last year, the“blockchian” was written into The 13th Five Year Plan - National Informatization Plan for the first time; earlier this year, launched by the People’s Bank of China, the blockchain-based digital notes trading platform passed the test. Tencent has been keeping its eyes on and making explorations in the blockchain space. In the first half of this year, the Tencent Blockchain White Paper, jointly compiled by the Tencent FiT (Financial Technology Group), Tencent Research Institute, Tencent Public Strategy Committee Office and Tencent Strategy Development Department, was released on the trustsql.qq.com.

Tencent blockchain industry solutions, with a number of the core patents, are developed independently. Compared with foreign blockchain technologies, Tencent blockchain program will be more secure and controllable in practical use. The program shows its technical advantages in high performance, high security, high speed access, efficient operation and other aspects since it is developed on the basis of the distributed system, massive payment capacity and high concurrent processing capacity of Tencent’s WeChat payment, QQ wallet and TenPay, as well as more than 12 years’ experience in running payment account and operating financial business.

Based on the principle of the "independent innovation, safety and efficiency, open sharing", Tencent takes advantage of its internal capacity to build the blockchain infrastructure. In the well-developed Tencent blockchain industry solutions, such core technologies as digital assets, smart contracts and verified certification will be applied to issue people’s financial life, such as robot investment advisor, aviation delay claims and the intellectual property protection.

“Blockchain can build a national credit system through a low-cost, high-efficient and transparent way, accelerating the development of inclusive financial system. At present, China is still in the early stage of exploring the blockchain technology. We expect to have more companies and institutions to join in the blockchain technology research and share their wisdom and achievements, promote the development of a reliable Internet and achieve a win - win situation, " said Guo Rui, deputy general manager of Tencent FiT.
