Ethereum's ERC-20 Token Standard Has Been Finalized

Editor: Xue Xu

11th Sept, 2017

The standard that governs the launch of new crypto tokens on the top of the Ethereum Blockchain has been finalized recently on the Ethereum GitHub page.

The tokens issued through Ethereum smart contracts need to be conform with a common set of rules under the ERC-20 standard, according to the open-source projectsâ€™ developers. It is also the basis for tokensale projects that have already been launched recently.

The standard guarantees that Ethereum-based tokens can perform in a predictable way in the ecosystem, thus an interoperability is ensured between decentralized applications and smart contracts of the platform. Token developers have adopted the ERC-20 standard since its introduction two years ago.

The standard comes as the roadmap for Ethereum's next major upgrade, Metropolis, becomes clearer. Developers recently suggest that a new testnet will be launched soon.
