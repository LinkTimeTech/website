The 3rd Global Blockchain Summit Successfully Held in Shanghai

Editor: Xue Xu

18th Sept, 2017

Three-day “The 3rd Global Blockchain Summit 2017” was successfully held in Shanghai on Sept.14-17, 2017. Hosted by Wanxiang Blockchain Labs, the Summit, with the theme of “Blockchain: New Economy—Breaking Dawn”, focuses on blockchain cutting-edge technology, blockchain application, policies and regulations, alliances and other important sectors.

As one of the biggest global events in the industry, the Summit attracted 1200 participants from all over the world. Decision makers, key leaders, representatives from financial institutions and some developers and scholars were invited to discuss about blockchains new economy. Vitalik Buterin, the co-founder of the Ethereum, Nick Szabo, the father of the smart contract, Patrick Byrne, the founder and CEO of Overstock, Daniel Larimer, CTO of EOS and other guests also attended the Summit and delivered speeches.

With the heat of blockchain remaining high in and outside of China, blockchain summits, big or small have been held one after another. On October 15, 2015, “Blockchain: Blueprint for a New Economy”, the first Global Blockchain Summit hosted by Wanxiang Blockchain Labs, was recognized as the first public blockchain event proper and also the monumental event of the development of blockchain technology in China, marking the year of 2015 as “the first blockchain year” in China.

From September 19-24th, 2016, Wanxiang Blockchain Labs and the Ethereum Foundation hosted a 6-day Shanghai Blockchain Week together, bringing the development and international status of the blockchain industry in China to a new level.

As the morning sun rises, glowing rays shine. Not dazzling though, the first light of the morning heralds limitless possibilities. The 3rd Global Blockchain Summit “New Economy—Breaking Dawn” was successfully held in W Shanghai-the Bund. It creates new opportunities for blockchain companies and teams that want to enter the Chinese market, and brings the development of blockchain to a new level once again in China. Wangxiang Blockchain labs is a non-profit frontier research institution funded by China Wanxiang Holdings with the focus on the blockchain technology. The labs will gather experts in the field to discuss the R&D of technology, business application and the industrial strategy, providing guidance for entrepreneurs and reference for industrial development and policy making, and to make blockchain technology a drive to the social economy.
