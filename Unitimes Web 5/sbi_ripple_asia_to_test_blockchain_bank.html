SBI Ripple Asia to Test Blockchain Bank Transfers Between Japan and South Korea

“Blockchain bank transfers test is just a beginning, what will happen in the upcoming future? Will this completely replace the existing bank transfer system? Let’s see!”

SBI Ripple Asia will begin a Digital Ledger Technology (DLT) based funds-transfer system test between Japanese and South Korean banks by the end of 2017, according to report.

SBI Ripple Asia is a joint venture built by Tokyo-based financial services firm SBI and a DLT payments startup Ripple. Earlier this year, the company has already led a consortium of banks to complete a pilot implementation with Ripple's technology within Japan. SBI Ripple Asia will work alongside DAYLI Intelligence (Blockchain and AI solutions provider) to expand the program into South Korea, according to the local media.

The company is now also planning a program which will commence in October, to train engineers from about 20 blockchain and cryptocurrency technologies companies, including Nomura Research Institute, Toppan Printing and NEC.
