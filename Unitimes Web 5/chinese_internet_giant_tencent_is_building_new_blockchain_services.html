Chinese Internet Giant Tencent is Building New Blockchain Services

“In the future, the Blockchain will lead the internet of information towards the internet of value to build a brand new trustworthy society”

Chinese internet conglomerate Tencent issued TrustSQL white paper in the first half of this year, aiming at building a suite of Blockchain services and working with its partners to push forward the development of a reliable internet. Meanwhile, the Tencent Bockchain industry solutions with independent intellectual property rights was released on its official website - trustsql.qq.com.

The Blockchain can store data in a secure and tamper-resistant manner, build trust among peers and reduce the cost via decentralization. In the future, the Blockchain will lead the internet of information towards the internet of value, reflecting the value of internet, making value transfering more transparent and thus creating a trustworthy society.

Late last year, the“ Blockchian” was written into The 13th Five Year Plan - National Informatization Plan for the first time; earlier this year, launched by the People’s Bank of China, the Blockchain-based digital notes trading platform was tested successfully. Tencent has been keeping its eyes on and making explorations in the Blockchain space. In the first half of this year, the Tencent TrustSQL White Paper, jointly compiled by the Tencent FiT (Financial Technology Group), Tencent Research Institute, Tencent Public Strategy Committee Office and Tencent Strategy Development Department, was released on the trustsql.qq.com.

Tencent Blockchain industry solutions, with a number of the core patents, are developed independently. Compared with foreign Blockchain technologies, Tencent blockchain program will be more secure and more controllable in use. The program shows its technical advantages in high performance, high security, high speed access, efficient operation and other aspects since it is developed on the basis of the distributed system, massive payment capacity and high concurrent processing capacity of Tencent’s WeChat payment, QQ wallet and TenPay, as well as more than 12 years’ experience in running payment account and operating financial business.

Based on the principle of the "independent innovation, safety and efficiency, open sharing", Tencent leverages its internal capacity to build Blockchain infrastructure. In the well-developed Tencent Blockchain industry solutions, such core technologies as digital assets, smart contracts and verified certification will be applied in people’s financial life, such as robot investment advisor, aviation delay claims and the intellectual property protection.

“Blockchain can build a national credit system through a low-cost, high-efficient and transparent way, accelerating the development of inclusive financial system. At present, China is still in the early stage of exploring the blockchain technology. We expect to have more companies and institutions to join in the blockchain technology research and share their wisdom and achievements, promote the development of a reliable Internet and achieve a win - win situation, " said Guo Rui, deputy general manager of Tencent FiT.
